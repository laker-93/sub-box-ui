Create an account with subbox.

The DJ tick box enables additional features on your account but will take longer to set up. Signing up for a DJ account will enable you to upload and stream your music collection privately as well as import/export from your DJ software to and from sub-box. Other users who intend to just listen to publicly available tracks on sub-box and upload tracks for public consumption can leave the box unchecked.

It will take sub-box a minute or so to set everything up so please be patient after clicking the create account button :)
