SUBBOX - ABOUT

Subbox is a service that allows you to upload your audio files and stream them anywhere.

When Subbox processes your audio files it will fetch meta information for the tracks, such as album art and lyrics so that this information appears in the music player.

Subbox also provides DJ software conversion utilities, so you can convert from your personal music collection to your DJ software. Read more about this feature here.
